    Certificates for Teacher Professional Development

    What is a Certificate?

    Overview

    Choose from a variety of non-degree certificates—and put the “Edge” in your professional development.

    A focused group of courses can make a lasting impact on teaching and learning, and open doors related to teaching assignment, leadership roles, and salary advancement.

    Learners Edge, in partnership with Augustana University and Pacific Lutheran University, is offering non-degree certificates.

    Certificate participants will receive:

    • Graduate credit from Pacific Lutheran University or Augustana University with certificate notation on their transcript (all courses within the Certificate must be taken through the SAME university partner)
    • Certificate of Completion

    Please note that these certificates/graduate credits are intended to fulfill requirements for license renewal/lane change when the student has obtained proper prior approval. The certificates are not intended for license endorsements or any change in licensure status beyond renewal.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    +How long do I have to complete the non-degree certificate courses?
    We strongly encourage you to complete the courses in the session in which you registered. If you are unable to complete the courses within 2 consecutive sessions, you will need to contact us to discuss options.
    +What do I gain by enrolling in a non-degree certificate?
    How a certificate is weighed is specific to every school district, or state. Earning a certificate demonstrates you have successfully completed a series of courses in a specific area.
    +Where does the credit for a non-degree certificate come from?
    Currently, credit for your certificate will come from either Pacific Lutheran University or Augustana University (you choose during registration). Both academic partners are regionally accredited. You must take all courses within a Certificate from the same university partner. 
    + Are discounts available when I order a Certificate?
    A multi-course discount is already calculated with Certificate pricing. Group discounts are able to be used with Certificate pricing instead of the multicourse discount. If you have a group code, please add your group code as you check-out. Additional promotions do not apply.
    +What else do I need to know to earn a non-degree certificate?
    • All courses must be taken through the same certificate granting academic partner.
    • Courses taken previously through a different academic partner do not apply.
    • Courses taken previously through Learners Edge do not apply
